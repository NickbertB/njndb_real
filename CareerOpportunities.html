Career Opportunities

Interested in joining our highly experienced and professional team? Look below for positions that are currently hiring at NJN Design & Build.

Head of Web Design

NJN Design & Build is seeking an experienced individual to serve as the company's Head of Web Design. This individual will be tasked with designing, implementing and maintaining the company's web presence, primarily through its website. The ideal candidate will have a strong, thorough understanding of all major web development languages, including HTML, PHP, JavaScript and CSS. S/he will also have a keen eye for design and will be capable of creating a coherent website for the company. All candidates must work well in groups and be open to both positive and critical feedback from colleagues and superiors.

This is a full-time position at NJN Design & Build's main offices near Boston, Massachusetts. Salary is determined by applicable experience and performance on pre-work challenges and exercises. Generous benefits are included with any position at NJN Design & Build.

If interested, please email resume and cover letter to Nicholas Newbold, CEO.

Building Manager

NJN Design & Build is seeking a dedicated individual to be the company's Building Manager. The company occupies two physical spaces: our offices in Boston as well as our warehouse and production facility nearby. The Building Manager would be in charge of both spaces. His/her main responsibilities would be to ensure upkeep of the spaces so all employees have safe and comfortable working environments, as well as to oversee a buildings and grounds team responsible for routine maintenance, including landscaping, and coordinating with outside contractors if a situation requires it. The ideal candidate has at least five years' experience in the field, as well as a deep understanding of common building systems and maintenance. S/he must be capable of performing strenuous labor if the job requires it, such as heavy lifting and maneuvering in tight spaces inside the building.

This is a full-time position at NJN Design & Build. The Building Manager will be commuting between the company's two spaces frequently, but has a dedicated office in our main building in Boston. Salary determined by experience. Generous benefits are included with and position at NJN Design & Build.

If interested, please email resume and cover letter to Nicholas Newbold, CEO.

Chief Marketing Officer/Director of Communications

NJN Design & Build is seeking a professional, energetic individual to be the company's Chief Marketing Officer (CMO). The CMO is in charge of designing, implementing and maintaining a successful marketing strategy for the company, in accordance with its mission and values. S/he will oversee creation of promotional material, which could include flyers and online and print advertisements. The CMO also has the title Director of Communications. In this capacity, s/he will be at the forefront of relationships between the company and third parties with which the company conducts business. Necessary qualifications include at least five years' experience in marketing, as well as possessing exemplary "people skills", including outstanding written and oral communication and the resolve to work demanding hours when necessary and to come to work each day ready to be as creative as possible.

This is a full-time position at NJN Design & Build's main offices near Boston, Massachusetts. Salary is determined by applicable experience and performance on pre-work challenges and exercises. Generous benefits are included with any position at NJN Design & Build.

If interested, please email resume and cover letter to Nicholas Newbold, CEO.
