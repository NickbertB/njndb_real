Prices & Fees

Build From Scratch

  1. The Consultation
    • A fee of $50 will be charged at the end of a consultation.
    • This fee can be paid with a credit/debit card or by cash.
  1. The Deposit
    • In order for us to start your build, you must pay a deposit.
    • The deposit amount will be 25% of the total cost set during the consultation.
  1. The By-The-Hour Charge
    • A fee of $25 per hour will be charged for the entirety of the build.
    • This amount is IN ADDITION to the total cost set during the consultation.
  1. The Final Product
    • When you take delivery of your new computer, you will pay the remaining 75% of the total cost.








Assist With Your Preexisting Build

We will charge a flat fee of $25 per hour for each hour we assist you with your preexisting build.
